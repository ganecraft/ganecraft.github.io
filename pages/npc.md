### NPCs

<div class="npc-div box-div">
<img class="npc-image" src="/assets/img/npc/npc1.jpg">
<p class="box-text">
NPCs are non-player characters that usually can be interacted with.
Most of them have dialogs, although there are NPCs whose primary function is just to exist as ambient entities.
They can't be moved, pushed, leashed or put in boats or minecarts.
NPCs don't take any damage and do not attack players, even if the NPC entity is a hostile mob.
</p>
</div>

### Function

<div class="npc-div box-div">
<p class="box-text">
Other than 'talking' NPCs can do things like trading items or teleporting the player.
The dialog (which is a book GUI) in this case has clickable buttons.
Keep in mind this usually costs items and/or money.
There are places which players can only reach when teleporting via NPCs.
</p>
<img class="npc-image" src="/assets/img/npc/npc2.jpg">
</div>
