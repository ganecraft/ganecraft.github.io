### Rarity change
<div class="box-div">
<p class="box-text">
Netherite gear are harder to make because they count as late mid/endgame items.
The amount of netherite required is much higher and it's difficult to mine in the nether, since the blast resistance of common nether blocks have been increased.
However, netherite (debris, scrap and sometimes ingots) can be obtained from mob drops and from boss or event chests.
</p>
<p class="box-text">
This results in netherite gear being more rare and makes netherite a sought after crafting material.
</p>
</div>

### Netherite upgrade
<div class="box-div">
<p class="box-text">
The gear upgrade process has been changed so netherite upgrade smithing templates have to be crafted and can not be found in bastion chests anymore.
Making one requires netherite and diamond blocks and also a trim template other than the upgrade template.
</p>
</div>
