Rarity change

Netherite gear are harder to make because they count as late mid/endgame items. The amount of netherite required is much higher and it's difficult to mine in the nether, since the blast resistance of common nether blocks have been increased. However, netherite (debris, scrap and sometimes ingots) can be obtained from mob drops and from boss or event chests.

This results in netherite gear being more rare and makes netherite a sought after crafting material.

Netherite upgrade

The gear upgrade process has been changed so netherite upgrade smithing templates have to be crafted and can not be found in bastion chests anymore. Making one requires netherite and diamond blocks and also a trim template other than the upgrade template.


The upgrade smithing templates can not be doubled, it always requires an additional trim template to create one.


Blast resistance of blocks in the nether is increased, and beds don't explode. With this change mining netherite via explosions is very tedious and probably isn't worth it with TNT.

Loot

Other than pick mining in the nether for ancient debris netherite can be obtained from the following:

Containers

Wooden box, Wither lootbag, Upgraded halloween chest

Mobs

Wither, Mobarena2 executioner, Mobarena3 mobs
